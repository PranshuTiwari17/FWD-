About This Recipe

This homemade pizza recipe gives you a cheesy and satisfying result with minimal effort. It is a great starting point for beginners, and you can customize toppings based on your taste.

Ingredients

  • 1 ready pizza base
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese, grated
  • 1/2 cup mixed vegetables (onion, capsicum, mushroom, tomato), sliced
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili flakes
  • A pinch of salt
  • Olives or jalapenos, optional

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 220 degrees C for at least 10 minutes. Place a baking tray inside while preheating if possible.
  2. Put the pizza base on a tray and lightly brush olive oil on the edges for a golden crust.
  3. Spread pizza sauce evenly, leaving about 1 cm border at the edges.
  4. Sprinkle half of the cheese over the sauce.
  5. Add the sliced vegetables evenly and season with a pinch of salt.
  6. Top with the remaining cheese, oregano, chili flakes, and optional olives or jalapenos.
  7. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until the cheese melts fully and the crust becomes golden.
  8. Rest for 2 minutes, then slice and serve hot.

Preparation Time: 35 mins

Difficulty: Medium
