About This Recipe

This chocolate cake is rich, soft, and perfect for birthdays, celebrations, or weekend baking. The method is simple enough for beginners while still giving a classic bakery-style result.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil or melted butter
  • 1 cup warm milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla essence
  • A pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 180 degrees C. Grease a cake tin and line the base with butter paper.
  2. In a bowl, sift flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt so the dry ingredients are evenly combined.
  3. In another large bowl, whisk eggs and sugar for 2-3 minutes until slightly pale and smooth.
  4. Add oil and vanilla essence to the egg mixture and whisk again.
  5. Add the dry ingredients in 2 batches, alternating with warm milk. Fold gently after each addition until you get a smooth lump-free batter.
  6. Pour the batter into the prepared tin and tap the tin lightly to remove trapped air bubbles.
  7. Bake for 30-35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  8. Cool in the tin for 10 minutes, then transfer to a rack and cool completely before slicing or frosting.

Preparation Time: 55 mins

Difficulty: Medium
