About This Recipe

Tacos are a fun and flavorful option for family meals or casual gatherings. This recipe keeps things simple so you can prepare a fresh and crunchy taco plate in very little time.

Ingredients

  • 6 taco shells
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 small tomato, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup sweet corn
  • 1 cup boiled kidney beans (rajma), lightly mashed
  • 1 teaspoon taco seasoning (or chili powder plus cumin)
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/2 cup shredded lettuce
  • 3/4 cup grated cheese
  • 4 tablespoons salsa
  • 2 tablespoons sour cream or mayonnaise, optional
  • Lemon wedges for serving

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan. Add chopped onion and cook for 2 minutes until soft.
  2. Add chopped tomato and sweet corn. Cook for 2-3 minutes until the tomato breaks down slightly.
  3. Add mashed kidney beans, taco seasoning, and salt. Mix well and cook for 4-5 minutes until the filling is thick and flavorful.
  4. Warm taco shells in a preheated oven at 180 degrees C for 2-3 minutes so they become crisp.
  5. Fill each shell with a little lettuce, then spoon in the warm bean filling.
  6. Top with grated cheese, salsa, and a little sour cream or mayonnaise if using.
  7. Finish with a squeeze of lemon and serve immediately.

Preparation Time: 20 mins

Difficulty: Easy
