About This Recipe

These wraps are light, quick, and very flexible. You can use whatever vegetables or sauces you already have at home, which makes this recipe perfect for a fast weekday meal.

Ingredients

  • 4 chapati or tortilla wraps
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (onion, capsicum, carrot, cabbage), thinly sliced
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  • 4 tablespoons mint mayo or any sandwich sauce
  • Lettuce leaves, optional

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan and add ginger-garlic paste. Saute for 30 seconds on medium heat.
  2. Add sliced vegetables and cook on high heat for 4-5 minutes so they stay slightly crunchy.
  3. Add red chili powder, garam masala, salt, and lemon juice. Mix well and cook for 1 more minute. Turn off the heat.
  4. Warm the wraps on a tawa for 15-20 seconds on each side so they become soft and easy to roll.
  5. Spread 1 tablespoon sauce over each wrap. Add lettuce leaves if using.
  6. Place the hot vegetable filling in the center. Fold the sides inward and roll tightly.
  7. Toast the rolled wraps seam-side down on a pan for 1-2 minutes for better texture.
  8. Cut in half and serve immediately.

Preparation Time: 25 mins

Difficulty: Easy
